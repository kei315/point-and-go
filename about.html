WHAT IS THIS?

Point and Go is a game where YOU decide the ending! Take control of the narrative through the choices you make!

HOW DO I USE THIS?

Begin your adventure by selecting the start button on the homepage. From there, you will be put into a situation and be given choices on how to proceed. Choose any of the options that appear in order to progress the story. Continue choosing different actions and see where it brings you!
